ABOUT

Engineer first. Everything else second.

I'm Nikola Pajović, an independent AI consultant based in Belgrade. I design and build AI systems — agents, RAG pipelines, LLM products — for clients who need them working in production, not in a slide deck.

Before consulting, I founded and built AgroB2B Hub — a live B2B trading platform for the frozen fruit industry — alone, from empty repo to press coverage. That experience is why I work the way I do: small scope, working software every week, ship or say why not.

When I'm not on client work I build my own systems — Telegram bots that write and publish blogs, and Hermes, a multi-agent SEO system I'm building in public.
